Lions Football Academy

Developing the Next Generation of Footballers in New Zealand

Lions Football Academy is a youth-focused soccer development program dedicated to nurturing talent, discipline, and a passion for the beautiful game. With a mission to empower young athletes through high-quality coaching and competitive play.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Safe Environment

Lions Football Academy is dedicated to providing a safe and enjoyable football experience for all children under 18, following The FA's safeguarding standards.

Thorough Vetting

All staff and volunteers undergo Enhanced DBS checks and follow strict conduct guidelines, including no unsupervised one-on-one time and maintaining appropriate boundaries.

Dedicated Support

Our Club Welfare Officer oversees all safeguarding matters. Any concerns about a child's welfare are taken seriously and reported to the appropriate authorities.

Safe & Supportive Environment

Our coaches are trained to create a positive and secure atmosphere for all players

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your football academy questions

We offer programs for players aged 6 to 18+ years old, with specialized training for different age groups:

  • 6-12 years: Youth Development Program
  • 13-17 years: Advanced Training Program
  • 18+ years: Elite Academy Program

Our training sessions are held:

  • Monday to Friday: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Sunday: Rest day

Additional match training on Saturdays

Yes, we provide:

  • Training balls
  • Cones and markers
  • Goalposts and nets
  • Practice vests

Players need to bring their own:

  • Cleats
  • Shin guards
  • Water bottle
  • Training attire

We offer a 2-week trial period where:

  • Players can experience our training methods
  • Coaches evaluate player's skills
  • Parents can observe the program

After the trial, we provide feedback and recommendations for the best program fit.

We use a comprehensive evaluation system that includes:

  • Technical skills assessment
  • Tactical understanding evaluation
  • Physical fitness testing
  • Mental game analysis

Regular progress reports are provided to players and parents.

Our academy offers:

  • Scouting opportunities for professional clubs
  • College placement assistance
  • International tournament participation
  • Professional development workshops

We have partnerships with professional clubs and scouts to help players reach their full potential.
